"Monty Python Star Eric Idle Opens Up About Financial Struggles: 'I Have to Work for My Living'"
"Monty Python" star Eric Idle, known for iconic works like "Monty Python and the Holy Grail" and "Spamalot," reveals financial struggles in a candid exchange. Despite past successes, Idle, now 80, continues working to support himself. Fans express admiration for his comedic talent and legacy. Idle remains active in entertainment, recently appearing on "The Masked Singer."
