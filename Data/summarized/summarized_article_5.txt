"Monty Python Star Eric Idle Opens Up About Financial Struggles and Continued Work in Hollywood"
"Monty Python" star Eric Idle, at 80 years old, reveals financial struggles despite the success of his iconic works like "Monty Python and the Holy Grail" and "Spamalot." Idle expresses the need to continue working for a living due to dwindling income streams, reflecting on his career and recent projects, including his participation in "The Masked Singer" in 2022.
