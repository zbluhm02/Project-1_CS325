"A Trio of Presidents: Biden, Obama, and Clinton unite for Historic Fundraiser in New York City"
President Joe Biden will stand alongside Barack Obama and Bill Clinton in a fundraising event in New York City, showcasing their unique relationship and solidarity. The three Democratic leaders have a shared history of cooperation and clashes as they work toward common goals, aiming to rally support for Biden's reelection despite challenges.
