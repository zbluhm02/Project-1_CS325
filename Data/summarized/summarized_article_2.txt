Title: Former FBI Informant Charged with Lying About Bidens' Business Dealings: Impact on Republicans' Impeachment Inquiry
Special counsel David Weiss has charged former FBI informant Alexander Smirnov with lying about Joe and Hunter Biden's involvement with Ukrainian company Burisma. The indictment refutes Republicans' impeachment claims. Smirnov faces charges of lying and creating false records and was arrested upon arrival in the US. Critics note the unreliable nature of Smirnov's claims, emphasizing the lack of credible evidence against the Bidens.
